About Me

Hi! I'm a rising junior studying CS at UC Berkeley, with an emphasis in data engineering. I am particularly interested in the applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning in engineering solutions to problems, but am always excited to explore new fields! I also love teaching CS and am really passionate about making the subject accessible to all!

In my free time, I love to 3D model cool designs using Autodesk Inventor and make amateur artwork out of recycled wires, gears, and resistors. Last year, I took apart an old printer to make a small art installation for my wall! I also enjoy hiking, biking (especially spin), and eating chocolate. :) Once, I finished a Trader Joe's pound plus chocolate bar in four days. (It was during dead week, so no regrets!)

Currently I'm working on developing a love for running long distances. I can't wait to get past the pain and finally achieve the famous "runner's high"! On a more technical note, I'm expanding my software engineering skills as a STEP Intern at Google for Summer 2020. Feel free to message me on LinkedIn or contact me at smruthibalajee@berkeley.edu to say hello!
